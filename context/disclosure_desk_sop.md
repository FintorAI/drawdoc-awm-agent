guidelines. o For VA Loans:

Certificate of Eligibility (COE).

 Ensure that the Upfront MIP (UFMIP) and Annual MIP are correctly
updated.  UFMIP is generally 1% of the loan amount and can be
financed into the loan.  Annual MIP is based on the loan's term and
amount.
5. Homeownership Education and Housing Counselling: Information must be
updated to generate Supplemental Consumer Information Form.
