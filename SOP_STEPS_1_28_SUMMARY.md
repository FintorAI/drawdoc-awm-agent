# DOCS DRAW SOP - Steps 1-28 Summary

## Overview
This document provides a summary of Steps 1-28 from the Docs Draw SOP (pages 1-104). The steps outline the complete process for drawing closing documents in Encompass.

---

## STEP 1: Initial Setup
**Title:** Before we start with verifying required forms, below are the primary details to verify with attached documents

**Key Actions:**
- Verify POA (Power of Attorney) and Trust status from Funding Custom fields
- Check Pricing overage adjustments (except VA Loans)
- Verify primary details before starting form verification

---

## STEP 2: Download Documents
**Title:** Download all required documents from "e-Folder" section

**Key Actions:**
- Download documents from Documents tab, organized by category
- Application Section: Initial 1003, Approval Final, MI Certificate, Borrower IDs, SSN Verification
- Government Section: FHA Case Assignment, VA Case Assignment, etc.
- Credit Section: Credit Report, Payoff statements
- Property Section: Title Report, Appraisal Report, Purchase Agreement
- Disclosures Section: Initial Loan Estimate, Last Disclosed LE
- Closing Section: Appraisal/Credit invoices, Closing Disclosure, Final 1003, Escrow Wire Instructions, etc.

## STEP 3: Document Organization
**Title:** List of documents to be downloaded & reviewed from Documents tab

**Key Actions:**
- Organize documents by category
- Check document tracking comments
- Verify document status (Ready to Ship = locked/certified)
- Verify LE certification status

## STEP 4: Borrower Summary - Origination
**Title:** First Form in Encompass where only highlighted details related to Loan is verified

**Key Actions:**
- Verify Borrower's Information: AWM Loan number, name, SSN, DOB, phone numbers, marital status, email
- Cross-reference with Final 1003, Approval Final, Driver's License, SSN Card/Verification
- Generate and verify MERS MIN number (check for duplicates on MERS website)
- Verify SSN matches across all documents
- Upload MIN search & SSN search results to Encompass

## STEP 5: Borrower Information - Vesting
**Title:** Next Form in Encompass where only highlighted details related to Loan is updated/verified

**Key Actions:**
- Verify borrower vesting with Processor (via Slack or vesting task)
- Update vesting information to match 1003 form and Title report
- Update AKA (Also Known As) names from Credit report, Fraud report, ID card
- Select correct Vesting Type (Individual, Co-Signor, Title Only, Settlor Trustee)
- Verify Occupancy Intent (Occupy, Will not Occupy, Already Occupy)
- Update Trust information if applicable (Trust name, Trustee Of, Trust date)
- Verify POA (Power of Attorney) status and update attorney name if applicable
- Update marital status and gender (required for vesting completeness)
- State-specific requirements (CA/NV: Married spouse verbiage, CO: No marital status required)

## STEP 7: Closing Conditions

- Copy PTF conditions from UW Conditions tab (only those starting with "PTF")
- Add PTF Condition for missing Signed Page-3 of Final 92900-A for FHA files (if not attached)

## STEP 18: Aggregate Escrow Account
**Title:** Next Form where escrow details are updated/verified

**Key Actions:**
- Set Servicer as All Western Mortgage Inc. (verify name & address)
- Verify 1st Payment Date (one month after Closing Date)
- Update Taxes, Special Assessments & Insurance: Monthly & yearly amounts from Tax Summary & HOI Policy

- Update Mortgage Insurance (when applicable): Upfront Premium, Funding Fee, Monthly MI

- Cushion: "2" months for Taxes & Insurance, "0" for MI
- Update Due Dates for Taxes & Insurance
- Update Mortgage Insurance Due Dates (12 months starting from first payment date)
- Verify Starting/Initial Balance stays positive
- Aggregate Escrow Adjustment should be Negative or ZERO

## STEP 19: 2015 Itemization
**Title:** Next Form where all fees and costs are itemized

**Key Actions:**
- Verify Loan Program & Closing Cost Program
- **Section 800 - Items Payable in Connection with Loan:**
  - 801 Our Origination Charge: Verify Lender's Origination, Processing, UW Fees from LE's/CD
  - 802 Your Credits or Points: Verify Lender Credit/Credit for rate chosen
  - Update Paid to name as "All Western Mortgage Inc."
  - Verify amounts in Borrower or Seller Column per LE & CD
  - Verify "Impacts APR" checked for Origination fees
- Continue with Sections 803-810 (various service fees)
- Verify all fees show in correct CD sections (A, B, C, etc.)
- Verify PAC (Paid at Closing) vs POC (Paid Outside Closing) designations

## STEP 20: Fee Variance

**Key Actions:**

- Applied Cure Amount (auto-calculated)
- Update Cure applied to Lender Credit (required cure amount from line 1)

## STEP 21: Closing Disclosure - 1
**Title:** First page of Closing Disclosure form

**Key Actions:**
- Verify Disclosure Information section (only needed for COC CD requirements)
- Handle 2nd COC CD requirement for Closing date extension
- Understand 3-day waiting period rules:
  - NO waiting period: COC CD with APR decrease or no cost changes
  - 3-day waiting period: COC CD with APR increase (>0.125%) or loan product changes

## STEP 22: Closing Disclosure - 2
**Title:** Second page of Closing Disclosure form

**Key Actions:**
- **Loan Costs:**
  - Section A - Origination Charges: Verify all Sec-A fees from 2015 Itemization
  - Section B - Services Borrower did not Shop for: Verify all Sec-B fees
  - Section C - Services Borrower Did Shop for: Verify all Sec-C fees
  - Section D - Total Loan Costs: Summary of A to C
- **Other Costs:**
  - Section E - Taxes & Other Government Fees: Verify all Sec-E fees
  - Section F - Prepaids: Verify all Sec-F fees
  - Section G - Initial Escrow Payment at Closing: Verify all Sec-G fees
- Use "Show More Lines" button if needed

---

## STEP 23: Closing Disclosure - 3
**Title:** Third page of Closing Disclosure form

**Key Actions:**
- **Summaries of Transactions:**
  - Section K & M - Due from Borrower at closing & Due to Seller at Closing
  - Line 4 - Add "Principal reduction to Borrower" or "Additional Payoff or debts" per UW Conditions
- Add Principal reduction via "Payoffs & Payments" button
- Add Additional debts/payoffs via Calculation Cash to Close >> Adjustment and Other Credits
- **IMPORTANT UPDATE on Payoffs:**
  - Final CD must reflect full creditor name and account numbers for required payoffs
  - Lenders Instructions to Escrow must identify ALL PTF conditions including debt payoffs
- Verify all transaction summaries

---

## STEP 24: Closing Disclosure - 4
**Title:** Fourth page of Closing Disclosure form

**Key Actions:**
- Verify **Loan Disclosures:**
  - **Assumption:** Check correct box per loan type:
    - FHA, VA, USDA & Fixed Amortization: "Assumption will allow"
    - Conventional & Fixed Amortization: "Assumption will not allow"
    - Conventional & ARM (specific investors): "Assumption will allow"
  - **Demand Feature:** Always "does not have a demand feature"
  - **Late Payment:** Verify 15 days late, verify charge % (Conventional: 5%, FHA/VA/USDA: 4%)
  - **Negative Amortization:** Always "do not have a negative amortization feature"
- Verify all disclosure checkboxes

---

## STEP 25: Closing Disclosure - 5
**Title:** Fifth page of Closing Disclosure form

**Key Actions:**
- **Loan Calculations:** Auto-calculated (verify APR is within acceptable range)
- Verify 3-day waiting period requirements if applicable
- **Other Disclosures:** Always check "state law may protect you from liability for the unpaid balance"
- **Contact Information:** Verify Lender, Real Estate Broker, Settlement agent details (from File Contacts)
- Use "Copy Form 1003" button if Lender info blank
- **Confirm Receipt:** Select Signature type as "By Name"

## STEP 26: Mavent/Compliance Review Report
**Title:** How to Run Mavent/Compliance Review Report before ordering Final Closing DOCS

**Key Actions:**
- Go to "Tools" >> "Compliance Review"
- Click "Order" button to generate new Mavent report (wait a few seconds)
- Check if failing on any Loan Status
- If fails, inform supervisor/team lead to review if fail is valid
- Once cleared/approved by lead/manager, proceed with Docs

## Notes

- **Process TAT:** 6 Business Hours from time loan hits DOCS order queue and all Closing Disclosure Status is updated as CD approved & ACK by Borrower(s)
- **Delays:** All Concerned Parties & Management to be informed 2 hours in advance regarding any delay
- **Document Status:** Verify "Ready to Ship" means file is locked/certified
- **LE Status:** Verify LE's are certified by QC team before proceeding
- **MERS MIN:** Always verify MIN number is unique and not duplicate
- **State-Specific Rules:** Various states have specific requirements (TX, CA, NV, CO, etc.)
- **Loan Type Specifics:** FHA, VA, USDA have additional required forms and verifications
